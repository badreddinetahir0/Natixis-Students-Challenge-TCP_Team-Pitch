# Natixis Investment Managers - The Students Challenge (Winning Team)

This repository contains the final pitch presentation and portfolio management strategy for our participation in **The Students Challenge 2024** organized by **Natixis Investment Managers** in Paris, France. 

Out of the competing global teams, our team, **TCP Investment Managers** (consisting of 6 students), emerged as the **Winning Team**, achieving a portfolio performance of **+3.57% over a 3-month investment horizon** while strictly adhering to risk and ESG constraints.

## 📊 Strategy & Portfolio Overview
As the Team Leader, I led the macro analysis, idea generation, stock picking, and asset allocation strategies for a virtual **$100M multi-asset portfolio**.

* **Investment Horizon:** 3 Months (Oct 2024 – Jan 2025)
* **Performance:** +3.57% absolute return
* **Core Mandate:** Multi-asset allocation (Equities & Fixed Income) utilizing advanced quantitative models and rule-based ESG compliance layers.

## 🛠️ Key Responsibilities & Execution
* **Macro Analysis & Asset Allocation:** Conducted top-down macro research to determine tactical asset allocation shifts between OECD equities and sovereign bonds.
* **Portfolio Optimization & Risk Management:** Implemented risk-budgeting models, tracking error constraints, and monitored volatility targets to optimize risk-adjusted returns.
* **Performance Attribution:** Conducted ongoing monitoring of performance metrics to ensure alignment with strategic investment objectives.
